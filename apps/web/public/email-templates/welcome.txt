01 — How the list grows

A number lands on the public block list when three different verified
accounts flag it inside a rolling 14-day window. No two accounts can
share a device fingerprint or IP block. That's the corroboration rule,
and it's why the list earns trust instead of asking for it.

02 — Your dashboard

Track which campaigns you've fought, see which of your reports
corroborated, and watch your first-flag credits accumulate.

03 — The iOS app

Install the Call Directory extension, flip it on in Settings, and the
corroborated block list goes live on your phone. Updates roll silently
in the background.
